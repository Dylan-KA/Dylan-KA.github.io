Dual Wield

Dual Wield is a first-person shooter developed in the Unity Game Engine as part of a group project. With 2 firearms equipped, use up to 10 unique weapon combinations to deal with any enemies you find.

It's the year 2124 and Earth faces imminent destruction from an alien invasion. As Johnny, your stereotypical Hollywood action-movie hero, players embark on a vital mission to defend our planet. With biceps as strong as a tank, wielding dual firearms, he is humanity's last hope against extinction.
