About the project

OnTaskPro is an iOS app I developed as part of an individual assessment, where I was tasked with brainstorming, designing, and building an app to address a real-world problem for a specific target audience. I chose to focus on individuals struggling with productivity and motivation. The goal of OnTaskPro is to help users stay on track by reducing distractions and boosting their drive to complete tasks. The app allows users to log their tasks, receive intelligent suggestions on what to work on next, and stay focused through a simplified, goal-oriented interface.

Technical Details

OnTaskPro was built using Swift and SwiftUI, and follows the Model-View-ViewModel (MVVM) architecture to ensure a clean and maintainable codebase.
